What is Longboarding?

Longboarding is a sport with a variety of different disciplines, but they all have one thing in common: a longboard! A longboard is like a skateboard, but is typically a little longer, with bigger softer wheels, and with trucks designed more for turning rather than grinding rails. Longboards can be used to cruise around town, race down hills, do cool slide tricks, and much more. Ultimately, no matter what you do on the board, it's about creative expression and having a lot of fun! If you're looking for something exciting that will challenge you physically, then longboarding is for you!

The KLA Community

Here in Kelowna and the Okanagan area, we strive to maintain an inclusive, welcoming environment for the new skater and experienced rider alike! We host regular meetups to ride together, and learn new skills, including how to slide and stop on a longboard. At any meetup, you'll find a group of like-minded individuals who are stoked to skate, stoked to help eachother learn to skate, and stoked to promote safe skating!

If you're looking to learn a new skill, want to dust off that old longboard in your garage, are seasoned skater who just moved into town, or anything in-between, then this is the club for you! Contact us to find out more and join our next meetup. We hope to see you soon!
